Theme of The Conference
The conference looks forward to submissions towards ‘Sustainable Practices in Civil Engineering and Applied Chemistry’, and is divided into the following sub-themes, but is not limited to:


1) Sustainable Practices in Structural Engineering
2) Sustainable Practices in Geotechnical Engineering
3) Sustainable Innovations in Transportation Engineering
4) Sustainable Treatment and Management options in Environmental Engineering
5) Hydrologic Modeling and Experimental Water Resources Engineering
6) Development of Innovative and Sustainable Materials used in construction
7) Remote Sensing and GIS Applications in Civil Engineering
8) Usage of Artificial Intelligence and Computing Techniques in Civil Engineering
9) Materials Science and Nanotechnology
10) Sustainable Practices in Material Chemistry
11) Sustainable Practices in Medicinal Chemistry
12) Recent advancements in Applied Chemistry
13) Polymeric Chemistry
14) Computational Chemistry
15) Industrial Chemistry
